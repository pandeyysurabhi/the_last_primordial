Before Time (Unknown)
• [[The Core]] comes into existence.
• [[The Core]] has no consciousness or morality; it simply reflects and amplifies choices.
• Reality exists in an unstable, lawless state.
The Age of Humanity
• Humanity develops advanced civilizations.
• Through ambition, humans discover [[The Core|the Core]].
• Different factions attempt to manipulate the Fundamental Laws for their own benefit.
• Reality begins collapsing.
• This event becomes known as The First Collapse.

• Each willingly binds themselves to one Fundamental Law.

The Era of [[Choice]]
• [[The Black Sword]] is created as a vessel to preserve the memories of every [[Choice Wielder]].
• The Seven appoint the first [[Choice Wielder]].

• Every one of them fails for different reasons.
[[Christ]]'s Era
• [[Christ]]'s father becomes the latest [[Choice Wielder]].
• He falls in love with [[Morvex]]'s immortal companion.
• [[Christ]] is born, violating the oldest law that no Primordial or [[Choice Wielder]] may pass on their legacy through
blood.
• [[Christ]]'s parents willingly accept execution to preserve the laws of reality.
• [[Christ]] survives and inherits [[The Black Sword|the Black Sword]] years later.
• He restores the [[Seven Realms]], uncovers the truth, reaches [[The Core|the Core]], and makes the Final [[Choice]].
