# Kingdom of Destruction
**Category**: [[Seven Kingdoms]] | **Primary Law**: [[Realm of Destruction]] | **Guardian**: [[Nihros]]

---

## Philosophy & Ideals
Unlike the others, this kingdom viewed destruction as necessary. They argued that without controlled destruction,
nothing new could replace the old. Their research attempted to control destruction rather than eliminate it.
Ironically, this made them one of the few kingdoms that understood balance better than most.
