# Spirit of Chaos
**Category**: [[Seven Realm Spirits]] | **Associated Realm**: [[Realm of Chaos]] | **Guardian**: [[Xael]]

---

## Corrupted Belief
> "Rules should never exist."
> Each Spirit is a corrupted manifestation of its corresponding Law. They represent what happens when a fundamental law of reality is taken to an unhealthy extreme.

## Narrative Encounter
Christ encounters and must defeat this Spirit during his trials. Details of this battle and its mechanics can be found in [[Vol III - Part 3 - 8. The Spirit of Chaos]].
