# Spirit of Time
**Category**: [[Seven Realm Spirits]] | **Associated Realm**: [[Realm of Time]] | **Guardian**: [[Chronar]]

---

## Corrupted Belief
> "Nothing should ever change."
> Each Spirit is a corrupted manifestation of its corresponding Law. They represent what happens when a fundamental law of reality is taken to an unhealthy extreme.

## Narrative Encounter
Christ encounters and must defeat this Spirit during his trials. Details of this battle and its mechanics can be found in [[Vol II - Part 4 - 3. The Realm Spirits]].
