# Spirit of Destruction
**Category**: [[Seven Realm Spirits]] | **Associated Realm**: [[Realm of Destruction]] | **Guardian**: [[Nihros]]

---

## Corrupted Belief
> "Everything deserves to end."
> Each Spirit is a corrupted manifestation of its corresponding Law. They represent what happens when a fundamental law of reality is taken to an unhealthy extreme.

## Narrative Encounter
Christ encounters and must defeat this Spirit during his trials. Details of this battle and its mechanics can be found in [[Vol III - Part 4 - 7. The Spirit of Destruction]].
