# Spirit of Life
**Category**: [[Seven Realm Spirits]] | **Associated Realm**: [[Realm of Life]] | **Guardian**: [[Elaria]]

---

## Corrupted Belief
> "Nothing should ever die."
> Each Spirit is a corrupted manifestation of its corresponding Law. They represent what happens when a fundamental law of reality is taken to an unhealthy extreme.

## Narrative Encounter
Christ encounters and must defeat this Spirit during his trials. Details of this battle and its mechanics can be found in [[Vol III - Part 2 - 10. The Spirit of Life]].
