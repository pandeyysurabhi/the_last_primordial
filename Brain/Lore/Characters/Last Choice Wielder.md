# Last Choice Wielder
**Category**: Lore Characters | **Father of [[Christ]]**

---

## Profile
The **Last Choice Wielder** before [[Christ]] was different from all his predecessors. Instead of viewing the [[Seven Primordials]] as enemies, he spent years understanding them and realized they were prisoners under an impossible burden.

He fell in love with the [[Immortal Woman]] who was saved by [[Morvex]]. Together, they concluded that the endless cycle itself was the true enemy, and they chose to have a child, deliberately breaking the oldest law of reality to give the future one final chance.

He was executed along with the [[Immortal Woman]] by the [[Seven Primordials]] to preserve the laws, but they hid Christ, and he left his memories inside [[The Black Sword]].
