# Immortal Woman
**Category**: Lore Characters | **Mother of [[Christ]]**

---

## Profile
The **Immortal Woman** was originally a mortal woman loved by [[Morvex]], the Guardian of Death. When her natural life ended, Morvex broke reality's oldest principle and prevented her death, granting her immortality.

Centuries later, she met the [[Last Choice Wielder]]. They connected deeply and decided to have a child, [[Christ]], carrying the essence of [[Choice]]. She willingly accepted execution alongside her partner to preserve the universal laws of reality, knowing their child would represent a final hope.
