## Profile
Represents: Memory and Change
Personality:
• Wise
• Reflective
• Exhausted
Theme:
Time should guide life, never imprison it.

## The Price of Becoming a Primordial (Narrative Context)
> [!WARNING]
> The Primordials are caretakers of reality, not gods. They carry a heavy personal burden.

Chronar experiences every moment simultaneously.
For him, the past, present and future all exist at once.
He remembers conversations that have not happened yet.
He mourns deaths that are still decades away.
He can never truly experience surprise.
