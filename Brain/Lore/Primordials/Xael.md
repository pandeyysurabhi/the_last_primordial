## The Price of Becoming a Primordial (Narrative Context)
> [!WARNING]
> The Primordials are caretakers of reality, not gods. They carry a heavy personal burden.

Chaos is not disorder.
Chaos is possibility.
Xael maintains uncertainty itself.
Without Chaos, every event would become predetermined.
Nothing new could ever happen.
Creativity.
Evolution.
[[Choice]].
All require a degree of chaos.
Among the Seven, Xael carries perhaps the most misunderstood responsibility.
Developer Note
Each Primordial's suffering should influence their visual design.
For example:
• [[Chronar]] may appear exhausted despite being immortal.
• [[Morvex]] should look emotionally distant rather than evil.
• [[Elaria]] should appear compassionate but constantly overwhelmed.
• Xael's appearance should subtly change over time, reflecting endless possibilities.
The player's first impression should never be "monster."
It should be "someone carrying an impossible burden."
