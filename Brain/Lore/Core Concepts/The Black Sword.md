# The Black Sword
**Category**: [[Lore Index|Core Concepts]]

---

## Profile
The Black Sword is not an evil weapon.
It serves three purposes:
1. Preserve the memories of every [[Choice Wielder]].
2. Judge the philosophy of its current bearer.
3. Guide humanity toward breaking the cycle.
After the Final [[Choice]], the Black Sword ceases to exist.
Its purpose is fulfilled.

## Narrative Context
The Black Sword is a mysterious weapon of unknown origin. It acts as a library of memories, collecting the failure, regrets, and philosophies of every [[Choice Wielder]] who has carried it over ten thousand years. When a new Wielder inherits it, they inherit a conversation spanning millennia. 

It awakens for [[Christ]] at the beginning of his journey, eventually releasing all its memories and ceasing to exist after the Final Choice.
