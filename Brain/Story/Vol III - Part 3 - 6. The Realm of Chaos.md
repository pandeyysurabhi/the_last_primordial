# 6. The Realm of Chaos
**Volume**: [[Volume III – The Pilgrimage Through the Seven Realms]] | **Part**: [[Part 3 – Death, Chaos, and the Cracks in Memory]]

---

If the [[Realm of Death]] is quiet...
the [[Realm of Chaos]] is overwhelming.
Every step changes the world.
Mountains melt into oceans.
Day becomes night.
Gravity reverses without warning.
Music changes constantly.
Even dialogue occasionally rearranges itself.
Nothing remains stable.
