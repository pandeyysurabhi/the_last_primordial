# 4. The Return of the Black Sword
**Volume**: [[Volume II – The Child of Choice]] | **Part**: [[Part 3 – The Black Sword and the Journey Begins]]

---

Far away...
hidden beneath the ruins of an ancient civilization...
[[The Black Sword|the Black Sword]] waited.
For years it remained motionless.
It rejected everyone who attempted to claim it.
Kings.
Mercenaries.
Treasure hunters.
None could even lift it.
Then...
without warning...
the sword disappeared.
At sunrise...
it appeared before [[Christ]].
No explosion.
No divine light.
It simply stood upright outside his home.
Waiting.
[[Christ]] approached cautiously.
The moment his hand touched the hilt...
the world fell silent.
