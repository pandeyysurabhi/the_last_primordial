# Part 3 – The Black Sword and the Journey Begins
**Parent Volume**: [[Volume II – The Child of Choice]]

## Introduction
Purpose of this Part
This section marks the beginning of the actual game.

It explains [[Christ]]'s life after his parents' death, his recurring nightmares, how [[The Black Sword|the Black Sword]] chooses him, and why
he sets out on his journey. This is also where the player's motivation is established: revenge against the Seven
Primordials.
Although the player believes the Seven are the villains at this point, the document should make it clear that the
player only knows [[Christ]]'s perspective, not the complete truth.

## Key Takeaways
• [[Christ]] grows up without knowing the truth about his parents' deaths.
• His nightmares are fragmented memories rather than ordinary dreams.
• [[The Black Sword]] chooses [[Christ]] when he is ready, carrying the wisdom and disagreements of every previous
[[Choice Wielder]].
• [[Christ]] misinterprets his parents' final words, strengthening his desire for revenge.
• Believing the [[Seven Primordials]] murdered his family, [[Christ]] leaves his village with one goal: to kill them.
• The spirit of the [[Last Choice Wielder]] (his father) is present within [[The Black Sword|the Black Sword]] but keeps his identity
hidden, guiding [[Christ]] only through subtle advice rather than direct answers.
