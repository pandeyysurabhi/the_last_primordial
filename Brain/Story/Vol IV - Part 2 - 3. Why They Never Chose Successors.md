# 3. Why They Never Chose Successors
**Volume**: [[Volume IV – The Core and the Final Choice]] | **Part**: [[Part 2 – The Burden of the Seven]]

---

[[Christ]] finally asks the question that has haunted him since childhood.
"If your burden is so terrible...
why didn't you simply give it to someone else?"
[[Chronar]] answers.
"We could have."
Silence.
"But then we would become exactly what humanity once feared."
Another pause.
"We chose this burden.

No one else should inherit it without making that same [[Choice|choice]]."
[[Christ]] suddenly remembers the oldest law.
No Fundamental Law may pass through blood.
Now...
he finally understands why.
