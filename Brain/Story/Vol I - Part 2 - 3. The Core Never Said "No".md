# 3. The Core Never Said "No"
**Volume**: [[Volume I – The Birth of Reality]] | **Part**: [[Part 2 – The Collapse of Reality]]

---

[[The Core]] possessed no morality.
It could not distinguish between wisdom and foolishness.

It simply responded to human intent.
Every successful experiment encouraged larger experiments.
Each kingdom modified a different fundamental law.
Initially, the changes appeared harmless.
Life expectancy increased.
Agriculture flourished.
Cities expanded.
Weather became predictable.
The world appeared to improve.
However, reality itself was beginning to lose balance.
