# 4. The Truth About His Parents
**Volume**: [[Volume IV – The Core and the Final Choice]] | **Part**: [[Part 2 – The Burden of the Seven]]

---

[[Morvex]] steps forward.
For thousands of years...
this is the conversation he dreaded most.
"I did not kill your parents because I hated them."
He lowers his head.
"I killed them because they asked me to."
[[Christ]] freezes.
[[Morvex]] continues.
"They understood what breaking the law would mean."
"They refused to run."
"They refused to fight."
"They accepted death...
so that you would never have to carry guilt for their decision."
[[Christ]] cannot believe it.
Then...
[[The Core|the Core]] shows the final memory.
His parents kneeling before the Seven.
His father speaks.
"Don't make an exception because we're your friends."
His mother smiles.
"If the law survives because of our deaths...
then let our deaths mean something."
The Seven didn't execute them out of anger.
They honored their final request.
