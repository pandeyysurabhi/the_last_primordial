# 4. Why Christ Was Spared
**Volume**: [[Volume II – The Child of Choice]] | **Part**: [[Part 2 – The Judgment of the Seven]]

---

The Seven never intended to punish [[Christ]].
He had committed no crime.
He had never chosen to be born.
Executing him would contradict the very principle the oldest law existed to protect.
Children should never bear responsibility for choices they did not make.
Instead...
the Seven decided to observe him.
If he truly carried the essence of [[Choice]]...
his own decisions would determine the future.
Not theirs.
