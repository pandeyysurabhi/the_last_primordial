# 10. The Spirit of Life
**Volume**: [[Volume III – The Pilgrimage Through the Seven Realms]] | **Part**: [[Part 2 – The Realms of Space and Life]]

---

The Spirit believes death is always evil.
It endlessly revives everything.
Nothing is allowed to die.
As a result...
The Realm becomes overcrowded.
Plants suffocate one another.
Animals compete endlessly for diminishing resources.
Living beings continue existing long after unbearable suffering should have ended.
Life without death becomes its own form of cruelty.
The Spirit cannot understand this.
