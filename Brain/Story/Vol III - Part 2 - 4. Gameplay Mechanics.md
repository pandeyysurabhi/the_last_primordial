# 4. Gameplay Mechanics
**Volume**: [[Volume III – The Pilgrimage Through the Seven Realms]] | **Part**: [[Part 2 – The Realms of Space and Life]]

---

The [[Realm of Space]] focuses on navigation and perspective.
Major mechanics include:
Impossible Architecture
Buildings connect to themselves.
Rooms exist inside other rooms.
Staircases loop endlessly.
The player must observe patterns instead of relying on instinct.
Dimensional Portals
Certain doors lead to completely different parts of the Realm.
The same doorway may lead somewhere different depending on the player's actions.
Perspective Puzzles
Objects appear different depending on where the player stands.
A tiny bridge viewed from one angle becomes a massive pathway from another.
The Realm constantly challenges the player's perception.
Gravity Shifts
Walking on walls.
Walking across ceilings.
Changing gravity to solve environmental puzzles.
Combat also adapts to these changing orientations.
Developer Note
The [[Realm of Space]] should feel mysterious rather than dangerous.
Players should frequently stop simply to admire the environment.
Wonder is the primary emotion here.
