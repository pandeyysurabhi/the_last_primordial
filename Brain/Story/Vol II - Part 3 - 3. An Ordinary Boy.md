# 3. An Ordinary Boy
**Volume**: [[Volume II – The Child of Choice]] | **Part**: [[Part 3 – The Black Sword and the Journey Begins]]

---

Despite his nightmares, [[Christ]] lived an ordinary life.
He helped villagers.
Worked in the fields.
Learned basic swordsmanship from the village guard.
Spent evenings listening to stories from travelers.
He laughed.
Made friends.

Dreamed about leaving the village one day.
Nothing about him suggested he would become the next [[Choice Wielder]].
This contrast is important.
The player should first know [[Christ]] as a normal human before witnessing his transformation.
