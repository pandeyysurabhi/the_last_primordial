# 1. The Realm of Creation
**Volume**: [[Volume III – The Pilgrimage Through the Seven Realms]] | **Part**: [[Part 4 – Creation, Destruction, and the Road to the Core]]

---

The [[Realm of Creation]] is unlike anything [[Christ]] has seen.
There are no ruins.
No signs of age.
Everything is constantly being born.
Entire mountains rise from the earth.
Rivers carve themselves into existence.
Stars form overhead before fading into daylight.
New plants bloom every second.
New creatures emerge from light itself.
The Realm never stops creating.
At first...
it feels beautiful.
Then...
it becomes overwhelming.
Eventually, there is no room left for anything.
New forests crush old ones.
New mountains bury ancient valleys.
New life replaces life that already exists.
Creation has become endless.
Philosophy of Creation
Creation is hope.
Every invention...
every child...
every dream...
every beginning...
exists because something new was allowed to exist.
But creation without restraint becomes consumption.
If nothing ever ends...
nothing new truly has meaning.
