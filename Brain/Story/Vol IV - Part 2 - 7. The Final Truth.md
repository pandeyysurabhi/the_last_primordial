# 7. The Final Truth
**Volume**: [[Volume IV – The Core and the Final Choice]] | **Part**: [[Part 2 – The Burden of the Seven]]

---

[[The Core]] finally reveals the one truth it had hidden.
The cycle was never created by humanity.
Nor by the Seven.
Nor by the [[Choice Wielder|Choice Wielders]].
It was created by [[The Core|the Core]] itself.
Not intentionally.
[[The Core]] simply mirrors choices.
Every [[Choice Wielder]] inherited the memories of previous ones.
Every generation carried the expectations of the last.
The more memories accumulated...
the fewer genuine choices remained.
The cycle repeated because no one was ever truly free.
They inherited too much.
[[Christ]] is different.
Because he was born before receiving [[The Black Sword|the Black Sword]].
He experienced love.
Friendship.
Loss.
Curiosity.
Life.
Before inheriting ten thousand years of memories.
For the first time...
[[Choice]] belongs to someone whose identity wasn't shaped entirely by the past.
