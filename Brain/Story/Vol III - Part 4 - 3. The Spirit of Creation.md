# 3. The Spirit of Creation
**Volume**: [[Volume III – The Pilgrimage Through the Seven Realms]] | **Part**: [[Part 4 – Creation, Destruction, and the Road to the Core]]

---

The Spirit believes creating more is always better.
Its philosophy is simple:
"If something can exist...
then it should."
It refuses to allow anything to disappear.
The battlefield itself continuously creates obstacles, weapons and enemies faster than [[Christ]] can destroy them.
Victory comes not from destroying everything...
but from finding the single source fueling endless creation.
When that source is purified...
the Realm finally becomes peaceful.
