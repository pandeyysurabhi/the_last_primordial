# 2. The Nightmares
**Volume**: [[Volume II – The Child of Choice]] | **Part**: [[Part 3 – The Black Sword and the Journey Begins]]

---

From early childhood, [[Christ]] experienced the same nightmare repeatedly.
He stood inside a burning home.
A black sword rested in his hands.
His parents called his name.
Seven enormous figures watched silently from the sky.
Every dream ended before he could understand what had happened.
The older he became...
the more vivid the dreams became.
Sometimes he woke with tears despite remembering nothing.
Sometimes he woke gripping an invisible sword.
Sometimes...
he heard voices.
Whispers.
Names.
Fragments of conversations.
He believed he was losing his mind.
Developer Note
The nightmares should evolve throughout the game.
Early nightmares are confusing and symbolic.
Later nightmares gradually reveal actual memories.
This allows players to slowly piece together the truth alongside [[Christ]].
