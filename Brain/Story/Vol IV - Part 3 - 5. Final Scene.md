# 5. Final Scene
**Volume**: [[Volume IV – The Core and the Final Choice]] | **Part**: [[Part 3 – The Three Choices]]

---

Regardless of the ending...
the final scene is always the same.
A young child discovers an ordinary black flower growing alone.
They pick it up.
They look toward the horizon.
Then smile.
The screen fades to black.
Final Narration
"Reality was never shaped by power."
"It was shaped by choices."
"Some choices created worlds."
"Some destroyed them."
"But every future..."
"...began with someone willing to choose."
