# 2. The Weight of Memory
**Volume**: [[Volume III – The Pilgrimage Through the Seven Realms]] | **Part**: [[Part 3 – Death, Chaos, and the Cracks in Memory]]

---

The souls within the Realm are not trapped.
They remain only because they have unfinished emotions.
Some regret never saying goodbye.
Some still wait for loved ones.
Some cannot forgive themselves.
Others cannot forgive the living.
[[Christ]] slowly realizes something disturbing.
Death is not suffering.
Regret is.
