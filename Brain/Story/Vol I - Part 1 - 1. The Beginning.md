# 1. The Beginning
**Volume**: [[Volume I – The Birth of Reality]] | **Part**: [[Part 1 – The Beginning of Everything]]

---

No one remembers how the universe was created. There are no myths, scriptures, or surviving records that explain
where existence came from. Even the [[Seven Primordials]] do not know the answer. The only thing older than every
star, every planet, and every living being is [[The Core|the Core]]. [[The Core]] is not a god. It has no emotions. No consciousness. No
desires. It does not judge good or evil. It simply exists. If the universe is a machine, then [[The Core|the Core]] is its engine. If
reality is a book, then [[The Core|the Core]] is the paper on which every page is written. Everything that exists ultimately depends
on it. Destroying [[The Core|the Core]] would not merely destroy a civilization—it would erase the very rules that allow reality to
function.
