# 8. The Spirit of Chaos
**Volume**: [[Volume III – The Pilgrimage Through the Seven Realms]] | **Part**: [[Part 3 – Death, Chaos, and the Cracks in Memory]]

---

The Spirit believes rules are prisons.
Its philosophy is simple:
"If everything changes...
no one can ever control anyone."
Initially, this seems like freedom.
But [[Christ]] soon realizes that endless unpredictability makes trust impossible.
Without some order...
friendships cannot form.
Promises lose meaning.
Civilizations collapse.
Chaos without balance becomes isolation.
