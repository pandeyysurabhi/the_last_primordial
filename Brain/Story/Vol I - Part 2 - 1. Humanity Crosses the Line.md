# 1. Humanity Crosses the Line
**Volume**: [[Volume I – The Birth of Reality]] | **Part**: [[Part 2 – The Collapse of Reality]]

---

For decades after discovering [[The Core|the Core]], humanity used it responsibly.
[[The Core]] cured diseases, improved agriculture, prevented natural disasters, and accelerated scientific advancement.
Every improvement reinforced the belief that humanity had finally gained control over its own future.
Eventually, a dangerous question emerged.
If [[The Core|the Core]] can change reality... what are its limits?
No one intended to destroy the world.
Instead, each kingdom believed they had discovered the next great step in human progress.
The mistake was not ambition.
The mistake was assuming reality could be perfected.
