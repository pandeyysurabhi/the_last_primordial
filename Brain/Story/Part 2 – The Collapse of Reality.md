## Introduction
Purpose of this Part

Kingdoms were not villains despite causing the catastrophe, and how the world's collapse led to the birth of the
[[Seven Primordials]].

## Key Takeaways

Humanity's downfall began with noble intentions rather than greed.
Each kingdom attempted to solve a genuine problem using [[The Core|the Core]].
The [[Universal Laws of Reality|Seven Fundamental Laws]] cannot be altered independently because they exist in perfect balance.
[[The Core]] faithfully obeyed humanity without judging whether its requests were wise.
The collapse of reality forced humanity to consider an irreversible solution.
Seven ordinary humans volunteered to sacrifice their humanity to preserve existence.
