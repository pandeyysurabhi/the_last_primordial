# 4. Meeting Aetherion
**Volume**: [[Volume III – The Pilgrimage Through the Seven Realms]] | **Part**: [[Part 4 – Creation, Destruction, and the Road to the Core]]

---

[[Aetherion]] appears surrounded by countless stars.
Unlike the others...
he smiles warmly.
He asks [[Christ]]:
"If you were given the power to create a perfect world...
would you erase the imperfect one?"
[[Christ]] remains silent.
[[Aetherion]] nods.
"Good.
A perfect world built upon forgotten lives is not perfection.
It is abandonment."
As he fades away, he quietly says:
"[[The Core]] has been waiting for you."
Those words stay with [[Christ]].
For the first time...
one of the Primordials sounds hopeful.
