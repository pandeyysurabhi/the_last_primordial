# 7. The Truth Waiting at the End
**Volume**: [[Volume II – The Child of Choice]] | **Part**: [[Part 4 – The Seven Realms]]

---

By the time [[Christ]] clears the Seventh Realm...

he has changed.
He began his journey seeking revenge.
Now he seeks answers.
The Seven finally invite him to the place where everything began.
[[The Core]].
For the first time...
[[Christ]] stands before the source of reality itself.
There...
the complete truth about his parents...
the Primordials...
the [[Choice Wielder|Choice Wielders]]...
and the endless cycle...
will finally be revealed.
But with that truth comes an impossible [[Choice|choice]].
Not whether to save humanity.
But what humanity truly deserves.
