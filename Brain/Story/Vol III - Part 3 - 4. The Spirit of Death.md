# 4. The Spirit of Death
**Volume**: [[Volume III – The Pilgrimage Through the Seven Realms]] | **Part**: [[Part 3 – Death, Chaos, and the Cracks in Memory]]

---

At the Realm's center waits the [[Spirit of Death]].
Unlike the peaceful souls around it, the Spirit has become convinced that existence itself is suffering.
Its conclusion is simple:
"If no one lives...
no one will ever grieve."
This is Death without compassion.
Not balance.
Not acceptance.
Only oblivion.
The battle reflects this philosophy.
The Spirit constantly drains [[Christ]]'s strength, forcing him to survive through patience rather than aggression.
