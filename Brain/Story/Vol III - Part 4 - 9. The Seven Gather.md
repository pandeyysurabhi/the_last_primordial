# 9. The Seven Gather
**Volume**: [[Volume III – The Pilgrimage Through the Seven Realms]] | **Part**: [[Part 4 – Creation, Destruction, and the Road to the Core]]

---

As [[Christ]] exits the final Realm...
something unprecedented happens.

Instead of disappearing individually...
all [[Seven Primordials]] stand together.
Not surrounding him.
Standing beside one another.
They do not bow.
Neither does [[Christ]].
For several moments...
no one speaks.
Then [[Chronar]] breaks the silence.
"Every [[Choice Wielder]] reached this point believing they understood reality."
He looks directly into [[Christ]]'s eyes.
"You are the first to arrive knowing you do not."
The Seven simultaneously open a gateway.
Beyond it lies a place unlike any Realm.
A place older than Time.
Older than Creation.
Older than the Primordials themselves.
[[The Core]].
