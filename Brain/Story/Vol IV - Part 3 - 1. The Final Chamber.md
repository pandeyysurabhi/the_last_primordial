# 1. The Final Chamber
**Volume**: [[Volume IV – The Core and the Final Choice]] | **Part**: [[Part 3 – The Three Choices]]

---

Beyond [[The Core|the Core]] lies a circular chamber unlike anything else in existence.
There are no walls.
Only an endless horizon of light.
In the center floats [[The Core|the Core]]—not as a glowing orb, but as a constantly shifting sphere made from countless
memories, voices, and possibilities.
Around it stand the [[Seven Primordials]].
They do not move.
They do not speak.
For the first time in ten thousand years...
they wait for someone else's decision.
