# 1. The Endless Cycle
**Volume**: [[Volume II – The Child of Choice]] | **Part**: [[Part 1 – The Impossible Child]]

---

For nearly ten thousand years, the cycle repeated.
A [[Choice Wielder]] would appear.
[[The Black Sword]] would find them.
They would learn the truth about reality.
They would eventually confront the [[Seven Primordials]].

And...
they would fail.
Some believed humanity deserved complete freedom.
Others believed the Primordials should permanently rule over reality.
Some tried destroying [[The Core|the Core]].
Others attempted to control it.
Every solution solved one problem while creating another.
The cycle always began again.
The Seven never interfered unless absolutely necessary.
Instead...
they watched.
Every [[Choice Wielder]] represented another possibility.
Another hope.
Another failure.
