# 2. The Weight They Carried
**Volume**: [[Volume IV – The Core and the Final Choice]] | **Part**: [[Part 2 – The Burden of the Seven]]

---

[[The Core]] reveals their memories.
Not grand battles.
Not heroic victories.
Just ten thousand years of endless responsibility.
[[Chronar]] – Time
Every second that passes...
[[Chronar]] experiences it.
Not one [[Timeline|timeline]].
Every possible [[Timeline|timeline]].
He has watched [[Christ]] die millions of times.
He has watched civilizations rise and disappear.
He remembers every conversation ever spoken.
He cannot forget anything.
Sleep no longer exists for him.
For ten thousand years...
he has never experienced silence.
[[Vastara]] – Space
[[Vastara]] feels every point in existence simultaneously.
Every collapsing star.
Every newborn world.
Every distant galaxy.
Every empty corner of reality.
She has never known what loneliness means.
Because she has never truly been alone.
[[Elaria]] – Life
Every birth fills her with joy.
Every illness hurts her.
Every dying child makes her cry.
Every flower blooming makes her smile.
She experiences every living thing.
Every moment.

Forever.
[[Morvex]] – Death
Every death passes through him.
Not only humans.
Every animal.
Every insect.
Every civilization.
Every star.
He personally escorts every soul toward peace.
He has cried more tears than all of humanity combined.
Yet...
he must never refuse anyone.
[[Nihros]] – Destruction
Every necessary ending scars him.
Every civilization that must fall.
Every mountain that must crumble.
Every dying sun.
He destroys not because he enjoys it...
but because new beginnings require space.
Every destruction leaves another scar upon his body.
[[Aetherion]] – Creation
Every new possibility begins with him.
Every dream.
Every invention.
Every hope.
Yet he knows...
many of those creations will eventually become weapons.
Still...
he continues creating.
Because hope is always worth another chance.
[[Xael]] – Chaos
Chaos constantly changes.
[[Xael]] has no stable identity.
Every thought alters him.
Every possibility reshapes his existence.
Sometimes he cannot even remember his own face.
He laughs...
because laughter is the only thing that remains constant.
