# 7. The Burden of Hope
**Volume**: [[Volume I – The Birth of Reality]] | **Part**: [[Part 4 – The Forgotten Law]]

---

The Seven slowly stopped believing the cycle would ever end.
Every [[Choice Wielder]] possessed courage.
Many possessed wisdom.
None possessed the answer.
The Primordials began to accept a painful truth.
Perhaps reality was never meant to be perfect.
Perhaps existence itself required uncertainty.
Yet they continued waiting.
Not because they expected success.
But because hope was the only thing they still possessed.
