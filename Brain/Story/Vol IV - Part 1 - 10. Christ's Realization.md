# 10. Christ's Realization
**Volume**: [[Volume IV – The Core and the Final Choice]] | **Part**: [[Part 1 – The Truth of the Core]]

---

Everything he believed was incomplete.
The Seven were not tyrants.
His parents were not helpless victims.
The [[Choice Wielder|Choice Wielders]] were not failures.
Even humanity was not purely evil.
Everyone had simply been trying to protect what they believed was right.
The true enemy...
was the endless cycle forcing every generation to repeat the same impossible decision.
[[Christ]] finally understands why he exists.
Not to defeat the Seven.
Not to destroy humanity.
Not even to destroy [[The Core|the Core]].
He exists because he is the first person born outside the cycle.
The first person capable of making a [[Choice|choice]] that was never available to those before him.
