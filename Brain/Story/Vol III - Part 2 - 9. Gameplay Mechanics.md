# 9. Gameplay Mechanics
**Volume**: [[Volume III – The Pilgrimage Through the Seven Realms]] | **Part**: [[Part 2 – The Realms of Space and Life]]

---

The [[Realm of Life]] focuses on growth and adaptation.
Major mechanics include:
Living Environment
Plants constantly grow.
Roots create bridges.
Flowers reveal hidden paths.
Trees reshape the landscape over time.
Symbiotic Creatures
Not every enemy attacks.
Some creatures assist the player.
Others react differently depending on how [[Christ]] treats them.
This introduces moral gameplay choices beyond combat.
Regeneration
Many enemies continuously heal.
Victory requires understanding their behavior rather than simply dealing more damage.
Ecosystem Puzzles
Removing one plant may unintentionally kill another.
Saving one creature may endanger another.
The player learns that every form of life is interconnected.
Developer Note
Unlike previous Realms, violence should rarely be the optimal solution here.
Encouraging peaceful interactions reinforces the Realm's philosophy.
