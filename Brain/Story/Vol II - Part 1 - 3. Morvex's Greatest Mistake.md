# 3. Morvex's Greatest Mistake
**Volume**: [[Volume II – The Child of Choice]] | **Part**: [[Part 1 – The Impossible Child]]

---

Among the Seven, [[Morvex]] carried the heaviest burden.
Every death in existence passed through him.
He watched children lose parents.
Parents lose children.
Friends lose one another.
Empires disappear.
Stars collapse.
He understood loss better than anyone.
Over thousands of years...
he became emotionally attached to a mortal woman.
She never feared death.
She accepted it as a natural part of life.
That acceptance fascinated [[Morvex]].
For the first time since becoming a Primordial...
he allowed himself to love someone.
When her natural life ended...
[[Morvex]] broke reality's oldest principle.
Instead of allowing her soul to move on...

he prevented her death.
She became immortal.
Not a Primordial.
Not a goddess.
Simply...
a human who could no longer die.
