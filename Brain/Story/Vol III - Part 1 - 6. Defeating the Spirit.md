# 6. Defeating the Spirit
**Volume**: [[Volume III – The Pilgrimage Through the Seven Realms]] | **Part**: [[Part 1 – The Realm of Time]]

---

Upon defeat, the Spirit does not explode or disappear.
Instead...
it smiles.
For the first time, its rigid expression softens.
"Time was never meant to imprison anyone."
Its body dissolves into countless fragments of light.
Those fragments spread across the Realm.
Immediately...
Time begins flowing naturally again.
The Realm heals.
This reinforces an important idea:
[[Christ]] is not destroying the Realms.
He is saving them.
