# 7. The Realm of Life
**Volume**: [[Volume III – The Pilgrimage Through the Seven Realms]] | **Part**: [[Part 2 – The Realms of Space and Life]]

---

The third Realm feels completely different.
There are no ruins.
No destruction.
No darkness.
Everything is alive.
Massive trees stretch beyond the clouds.
Flowers bloom with every step.
Creatures of every shape coexist peacefully.
At first...
the Realm appears perfect.
Too perfect.
