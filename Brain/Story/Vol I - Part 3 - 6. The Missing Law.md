# 6. The Missing Law
**Volume**: [[Volume I – The Birth of Reality]] | **Part**: [[Part 3 – The Seven Primordials]]

---

While studying the balance of existence, the Seven made a shocking discovery.
Reality did not originally consist of only seven laws.
There had once been another.
One that no longer existed among them.
A law unlike the others.
One that no guardian had inherited.
One that could not be predicted.
One that could not be controlled.
It was known only as...
[[Choice]].
Unlike Creation or Time, [[Choice]] refused to remain constant.
It existed wherever free will existed.
It could not be permanently assigned to a single guardian.
It rejected permanence itself.
The Seven now understood why history continued repeating.
As long as [[Choice]] existed...
the possibility of another catastrophe would always remain.
