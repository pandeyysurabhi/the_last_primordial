# 6. Gameplay Mechanics
**Volume**: [[Volume III – The Pilgrimage Through the Seven Realms]] | **Part**: [[Part 4 – Creation, Destruction, and the Road to the Core]]

---

Unlike previous Realms, resources are limited.
Weapons deteriorate.
Platforms collapse permanently.
Safe zones disappear over time.
Players must decide carefully:
• Which paths to preserve.
• Which bridges to sacrifice.
• Which tools deserve repairing.
• Which battles are worth fighting.
The Realm constantly asks:
"What are you willing to let go?"
