# 10. The Cracks in Memory
**Volume**: [[Volume III – The Pilgrimage Through the Seven Realms]] | **Part**: [[Part 3 – Death, Chaos, and the Cracks in Memory]]

---

As [[Christ]] leaves the [[Realm of Chaos]]...
[[The Black Sword|the Black Sword]] suddenly reacts violently.
Every previous [[Choice Wielder]] begins speaking at once.
The voices overlap.
Contradict each other.
Argue.
Scream.
Then...
everything stops.
[[Christ]] sees something he has never seen before.
Not a dream.
Not a vision.
A memory.
His father...
standing beside [[Morvex]].
Talking.
Laughing.
Like old friends.
[[Christ]] immediately rejects the memory.
It cannot be real.
[[Morvex]] murdered his parents.
Why would they ever smile at one another?
The Sword offers no explanation.
Only silence.
