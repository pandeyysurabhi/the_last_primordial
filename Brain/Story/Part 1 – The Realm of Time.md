# Part 1 – The Realm of Time
**Parent Volume**: [[Volume III – The Pilgrimage Through the Seven Realms]]

## Introduction
Purpose of this Part
This section begins [[Christ]]'s pilgrimage through the [[Seven Realms]].
The [[Realm of Time]] serves as the player's first major test. Rather than simply introducing a boss fight, it teaches the
player that every Realm is a lesson about a Fundamental Law.
This is also the first time [[Christ]] meets one of the [[Seven Primordials]] face-to-face.

## Key Takeaways
• The [[Realm of Time]] introduces the philosophy behind the [[Seven Realms]].
• Realm Spirits are corrupted manifestations of their Laws and must be freed, not simply defeated.
• [[Chronar]] chooses conversation over combat, immediately challenging [[Christ]]'s belief that the Primordials are
monsters.
• Every Realm combines gameplay, environmental storytelling, and philosophical themes.
• [[Christ]] completes his first trial, but leaves with more doubts than certainty.
