# 8. The First Guide
**Volume**: [[Volume II – The Child of Choice]] | **Part**: [[Part 3 – The Black Sword and the Journey Begins]]

---

As [[Christ]] crossed the village border, one voice within [[The Black Sword|the Black Sword]] became clearer than the others.
It belonged to the [[Last Choice Wielder]]—his father.
However, [[Christ]] didn't recognize it.
The voice refused to reveal its identity.
Instead, it gave only one warning:
"Hatred makes decisions easy. Truth makes them difficult."
Before [[Christ]] could ask another question...
the voice disappeared.
He dismissed it as another hallucination.

But from that moment onward, his father's spirit quietly watched over him, choosing not to interfere unless
absolutely necessary.
