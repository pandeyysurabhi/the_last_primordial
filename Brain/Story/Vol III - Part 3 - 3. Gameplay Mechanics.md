# 3. Gameplay Mechanics
**Volume**: [[Volume III – The Pilgrimage Through the Seven Realms]] | **Part**: [[Part 3 – Death, Chaos, and the Cracks in Memory]]

---

Instead of combat-heavy gameplay, the Realm focuses on emotional encounters.
Players help souls move on by resolving their lingering regrets.
Examples include:
• Returning forgotten memories.
• Delivering final messages.
• Helping two spirits reconcile after centuries.
• Allowing someone to finally accept their own death.
Every soul that finds peace restores a fragment of the Realm.
Developer Note
This Realm intentionally slows the pace of the game.
Players should spend time reflecting rather than fighting.
