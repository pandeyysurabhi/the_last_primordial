# 1. The Realm of Death
**Volume**: [[Volume III – The Pilgrimage Through the Seven Realms]] | **Part**: [[Part 3 – Death, Chaos, and the Cracks in Memory]]

---

Unlike the previous Realms, the [[Realm of Death]] is completely silent.
No wind.
No animals.
No enemies waiting in ambush.
Only an endless white landscape stretching toward the horizon.
At first glance...
it appears empty.
But as [[Christ]] walks farther...
figures begin appearing in the distance.
They are people.
Some wave at him.
Some cry.
Some simply watch.

Every one of them is already dead.
Developer Note
The [[Realm of Death]] should not feel frightening.
It should feel peaceful.
The goal is to challenge the player's assumptions about death itself.
