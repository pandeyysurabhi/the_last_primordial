# 9. The Core Speaks
**Volume**: [[Volume IV – The Core and the Final Choice]] | **Part**: [[Part 1 – The Truth of the Core]]

---

For the first time in history...
[[The Core|the Core]] communicates.
Not with words.
With understanding.
[[Christ]] suddenly realizes the truth.
[[The Core]] has never manipulated anyone.
Never judged anyone.
Never chosen heroes.
It simply responds to the choices freely made by living beings.
It has no desires.
No morality.
No ambition.
It is a mirror.
The world has always reflected the choices of those who shape it.
