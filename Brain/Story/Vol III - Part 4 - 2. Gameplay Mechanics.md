# 2. Gameplay Mechanics
**Volume**: [[Volume III – The Pilgrimage Through the Seven Realms]] | **Part**: [[Part 4 – Creation, Destruction, and the Road to the Core]]

---

The Realm constantly generates new environments.
No area remains identical for long.
Examples:
• Bridges grow while the player is crossing them.
• Weapons evolve with continued use.
• New enemies are born during combat.
• Entire puzzle layouts regenerate if the player waits too long.
Players are encouraged to keep moving forward rather than remaining comfortable.
