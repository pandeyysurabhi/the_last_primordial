# 2. The Ascension
**Volume**: [[Volume I – The Birth of Reality]] | **Part**: [[Part 3 – The Seven Primordials]]

---

The Seven entered [[The Core|the Core]] together.
No one outside the chamber witnessed what happened.
The process lasted only a few moments.
When the chamber finally opened, the seven volunteers emerged transformed.
Their bodies no longer aged.
Their physical forms had become manifestations of their respective laws.
Reality immediately began stabilizing.
Time resumed its natural flow.
Space repaired itself.
Life and Death returned to balance.
The universe had survived.
Humanity celebrated.
The Seven did not.
They immediately understood the true cost of what had happened.
