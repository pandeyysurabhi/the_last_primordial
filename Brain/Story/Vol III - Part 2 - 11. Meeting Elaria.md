# 11. Meeting Elaria
**Volume**: [[Volume III – The Pilgrimage Through the Seven Realms]] | **Part**: [[Part 2 – The Realms of Space and Life]]

---

After restoring balance, the endless growth begins slowing naturally.
The Realm finally breathes again.
[[Elaria]] appears among blooming flowers.
Unlike the other Primordials...
she looks genuinely happy to see [[Christ]] alive.
She asks only one question.
"Would you save every life...
even if saving one meant condemning thousands?"
[[Christ]] cannot answer.

For the first time...
he remains completely silent.
[[Elaria]] nods.
"Good.
Some questions should never have easy answers."
She disappears.
