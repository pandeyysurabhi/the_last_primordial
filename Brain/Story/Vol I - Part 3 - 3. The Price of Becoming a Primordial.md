# 3. The Price of Becoming a Primordial
**Volume**: [[Volume I – The Birth of Reality]] | **Part**: [[Part 3 – The Seven Primordials]]

---

The Seven had not gained immortality.
They had lost the ability to live.
Each Primordial now experienced existence differently.
[[Chronar]] – Time
[[Chronar]] experiences every moment simultaneously.
For him, the past, present and future all exist at once.
He remembers conversations that have not happened yet.
He mourns deaths that are still decades away.
He can never truly experience surprise.
[[Vastara]] – Space
[[Vastara]] constantly maintains the structure of reality.
Every tear in space causes immense pain.
She cannot rest.
Even while speaking to others, part of her consciousness is repairing reality across the universe.
[[Elaria]] – Life
[[Elaria]] feels every living being.
Every birth.
Every illness.

Every heartbeat.
Every act of love.
Every moment of suffering.
She experiences all of it simultaneously.
Saving one life means witnessing thousands of others die elsewhere.
[[Morvex]] – Death
[[Morvex]] experiences every death.
Every final breath.
Every goodbye.
Every parent losing a child.
Every child losing a parent.
He is not responsible for causing death.
He simply bears witness to every single one.
Over thousands of years, grief becomes his constant companion.
etherion – Creation
Creation never ends.
Stars are born.
Worlds form.
New life appears constantly.
[[Aetherion]]'s responsibility is to ensure creation never overwhelms reality.
Without destruction, endless creation would eventually consume existence.
[[Nihros]] – Destruction
Unlike humanity's beliefs, destruction is not evil.
Old stars must die for new ones to exist.
Dead forests allow new ecosystems to grow.
Broken civilizations create opportunities for better ones.
[[Nihros]] ensures destruction remains balanced rather than excessive.
He is hated for a responsibility that reality cannot survive without.
[[Xael]] – Chaos
Chaos is not disorder.
Chaos is possibility.
[[Xael]] maintains uncertainty itself.
Without Chaos, every event would become predetermined.
Nothing new could ever happen.
Creativity.
Evolution.
[[Choice]].
All require a degree of chaos.
Among the Seven, [[Xael]] carries perhaps the most misunderstood responsibility.
Developer Note
Each Primordial's suffering should influence their visual design.
For example:
• [[Chronar]] may appear exhausted despite being immortal.
• [[Morvex]] should look emotionally distant rather than evil.
• [[Elaria]] should appear compassionate but constantly overwhelmed.
• [[Xael]]'s appearance should subtly change over time, reflecting endless possibilities.
The player's first impression should never be "monster."
It should be "someone carrying an impossible burden."
