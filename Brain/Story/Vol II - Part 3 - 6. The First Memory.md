# 6. The First Memory
**Volume**: [[Volume II – The Child of Choice]] | **Part**: [[Part 3 – The Black Sword and the Journey Begins]]

---

The moment [[Christ]] accepted the sword...
his recurring nightmare changed.
For the first time...
it continued beyond the point where it had always ended.
He saw his parents clearly.
He saw towering silhouettes standing over them.
He heard one final sentence before waking.
"Forgive us... one day, you'll understand."
[[Christ]] misunderstood those words.
He believed his parents were asking forgiveness because they had failed to protect him.
In reality...
they were asking forgiveness for the burden they had placed upon him.
This misunderstanding becomes the emotional foundation of [[Christ]]'s hatred toward the Seven.
