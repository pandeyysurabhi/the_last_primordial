# 3. Why Choice Could Never Have A Guardian
**Volume**: [[Volume I – The Birth of Reality]] | **Part**: [[Part 4 – The Forgotten Law]]

---

The Seven initially believed someone could simply inherit [[Choice]] like the other laws.
Every attempt failed.

Unlike Time or Death...
[[Choice]] rejected permanence.
Whenever someone tried to bind it,
it disappeared.
Whenever someone tried to control it,
it resurfaced somewhere else.
The Seven realized something disturbing.
[[Choice]] wasn't meant to be owned.
It was meant to be experienced.
