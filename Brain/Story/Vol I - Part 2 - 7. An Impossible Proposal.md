# 7. An Impossible Proposal
**Volume**: [[Volume I – The Birth of Reality]] | **Part**: [[Part 2 – The Collapse of Reality]]

---

Among countless scholars stood one researcher whose conclusion shocked the council.
[[The Core]] could not repair reality.
Only the Fundamental Laws themselves could.
But the laws had no consciousness.
No guardians.
No one maintaining their balance.
His proposal seemed absurd.
If someone permanently fused themselves with each law...
Reality might stabilize again.
The cost was obvious.
Those individuals would cease being human forever.
No one volunteered.
Until seven people stepped forward.
None of them were rulers.
None were chosen by prophecy.
They simply believed that if someone had to bear eternity...
it should be them.
