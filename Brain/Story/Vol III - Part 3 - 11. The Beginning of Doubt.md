# 11. The Beginning of Doubt
**Volume**: [[Volume III – The Pilgrimage Through the Seven Realms]] | **Part**: [[Part 3 – Death, Chaos, and the Cracks in Memory]]

---

For the first time since leaving home...
[[Christ]] questions whether his memories are complete.
Had someone altered them?
Had he misunderstood them?
Or...
had he built his entire life around an incomplete truth?
The player ends this section asking the same question.
Were the Primordials ever the true enemy?
Or has [[Christ]] been chasing the wrong target from the very beginning?
