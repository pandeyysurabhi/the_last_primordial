# 1. The Seven Volunteers
**Volume**: [[Volume I – The Birth of Reality]] | **Part**: [[Part 3 – The Seven Primordials]]

---

The proposal made during the Final Council required seven individuals to permanently merge with the Seven
Fundamental Laws.
This was not a ritual that granted power.
It was a sacrifice.

The volunteers would lose their mortality, individuality, and freedom. Their lives would become inseparably linked to
the law they inherited. If that law weakened, they would weaken. If reality suffered, they would suffer with it.
The council did not force anyone to accept this burden.
Seven people chose it willingly.
History no longer remembers their original names.
Over thousands of years, they became known only by the names of the laws they carried.
• [[Aetherion]] – Guardian of Creation
• [[Nihros]] – Guardian of Destruction
• [[Chronar]] – Guardian of Time
• [[Vastara]] – Guardian of Space
• [[Elaria]] – Guardian of Life
• [[Morvex]] – Guardian of Death
• [[Xael]] – Guardian of Chaos
Developer Note
The Primordials were ordinary humans before the sacrifice.
This is important because the player should eventually realize that the Seven are not divine beings—they are
ordinary people who made the greatest sacrifice in history.
