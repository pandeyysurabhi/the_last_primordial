# 9. Xael
**Volume**: [[Volume III – The Pilgrimage Through the Seven Realms]] | **Part**: [[Part 3 – Death, Chaos, and the Cracks in Memory]]

---

[[Xael]] appears laughing.
Not because he enjoys suffering.
Because every conversation with [[Christ]] unfolds differently.
He is the only Primordial who genuinely cannot predict what [[Christ]] will say next.
Instead of asking a question...
[[Xael]] asks [[Christ]] to choose.
Two identical doors appear.
"There is no correct answer.
Choose."

[[Christ]] selects one.
Both doors lead to the same destination.
[[Xael]] smiles.
"The destination never mattered.
Only the fact that you were free to choose."
Then he disappears.
