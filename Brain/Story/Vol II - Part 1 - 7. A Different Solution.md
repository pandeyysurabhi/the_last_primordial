# 7. A Different Solution
**Volume**: [[Volume II – The Child of Choice]] | **Part**: [[Part 1 – The Impossible Child]]

---

The [[Last Choice Wielder]] began questioning this law.
Not because he wanted power.
Not because he wanted a family.
Because he believed every previous [[Choice Wielder]] had begun their journey too late.
What if...
instead of waiting for [[Choice]] to appear...
someone was born carrying its essence?
Someone who grew up questioning reality instead of discovering it halfway through life.
Someone capable of breaking the cycle before repeating it.
The [[Immortal Woman|immortal woman]] understood the consequences immediately.
If they had a child...
they would violate the oldest law in existence.
The Seven would have no [[Choice|choice]] but to intervene.
