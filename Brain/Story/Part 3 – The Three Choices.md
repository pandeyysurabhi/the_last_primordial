# Part 3 – The Three Choices
**Parent Volume**: [[Volume IV – The Core and the Final Choice]]

## Introduction
Purpose of this Part
This chapter is the climax of the entire story.
After learning the truth, [[Christ]] must make the greatest decision in history. There is no "good" or "bad" ending. Each
ending reflects a different philosophy, and each comes with its own sacrifice.
The player's choices throughout the game—how they treated NPCs, solved dilemmas, and responded to the
Primordials' questions—unlock or influence these endings.

## Key Takeaways
• [[The Black Sword]] completes its purpose and releases every previous [[Choice Wielder]].
• [[Christ]] alone is entrusted with the fate of reality.
• The player chooses one of three philosophical endings: sacrifice, freedom, or shared responsibility.
• No ending is presented as objectively correct; each reflects a different worldview.
• The true ending, "The Broken Cycle," fulfills the central theme established from the beginning of the story:
the greatest burden should never belong to one person alone.
