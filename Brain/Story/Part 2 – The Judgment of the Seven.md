# Part 2 – The Judgment of the Seven
**Parent Volume**: [[Volume II – The Child of Choice]]

## Introduction
Purpose of this Part
This section explains how the [[Seven Primordials]] discovered [[Christ]]'s existence, why they were forced to execute his
parents despite understanding their intentions, and how [[The Black Sword|the Black Sword]] ultimately reached [[Christ]].
This is the emotional turning point that creates the central conflict of the game.

## Key Takeaways
• The Seven discover [[Christ]] through disturbances in the Fundamental Laws.
• They debate his fate rather than immediately enforcing the law.
• Every Primordial has a different perspective, highlighting their personalities.
• [[Christ]] is spared because the Seven refuse to punish a child for his parents' choices.
• His parents willingly accept their execution, believing [[Christ]] represents humanity's final hope.
• Before dying, [[Christ]]'s father releases [[The Black Sword|the Black Sword]], ensuring it will one day find [[Christ]].
• [[Christ]] grows up haunted by fragmented memories that become the game's opening nightmare.
