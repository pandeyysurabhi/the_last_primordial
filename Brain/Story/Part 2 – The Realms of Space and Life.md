# Part 2 – The Realms of Space and Life
**Parent Volume**: [[Volume III – The Pilgrimage Through the Seven Realms]]

## Introduction
Purpose of this Part
This section covers [[Christ]]'s journey through the second and third Realms—Space and Life.
These Realms are designed to shift [[Christ]]'s perspective. Until now, he believes strength is enough to complete his
mission. These two Realms begin teaching him that understanding a Law is far more important than defeating it.
The player should also begin questioning whether the Primordials are truly the villains of the story.

## Key Takeaways
• [[The Black Sword]] evolves alongside [[Christ]], recording not just his actions but the philosophy behind them.
• The [[Realm of Space]] teaches that freedom requires uncertainty and that perfect order becomes a prison.
• The [[Realm of Life]] demonstrates that preserving life without accepting death ultimately creates suffering.
• [[Vastara]] and [[Elaria]] continue the Primordials' pattern of guiding [[Christ]] through questions rather than battles.
• [[Christ]] begins to recognize that the Seven are not behaving like the monsters he imagined.
• His journey gradually shifts from one of revenge to one of understanding, laying the groundwork for the
emotional and philosophical conflicts that will dominate the remainder of the game.
