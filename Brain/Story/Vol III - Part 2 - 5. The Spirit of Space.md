# 5. The Spirit of Space
**Volume**: [[Volume III – The Pilgrimage Through the Seven Realms]] | **Part**: [[Part 2 – The Realms of Space and Life]]

---

The Spirit believes every corner of reality should be perfectly organized.
Nothing should ever move.
Nothing should ever change location.
Everything belongs exactly where it already exists.
To the Spirit...
freedom creates disorder.
Its philosophy is:
"A perfect world has no uncertainty."
The Spirit attempts to imprison [[Christ]] inside endless geometric structures where every path eventually returns to the
beginning.
