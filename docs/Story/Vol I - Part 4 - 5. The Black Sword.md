# 5. The Black Sword
**Volume**: [[Volume I – The Birth of Reality]] | **Part**: [[Part 4 – The Forgotten Law]]

---

[[The Black Sword]] did not grant power.
It granted perspective.
Every [[Choice Wielder]] left behind their memories inside the blade.
When the next Wielder inherited it,
they inherited every previous failure.
Every regret.
Every philosophy.
Every unanswered question.
The sword became less of a weapon...
and more of a conversation spanning thousands of years.
Some voices believed humanity deserved another chance.
Others believed the Seven should rule forever.
Some argued [[The Core|the Core]] should be destroyed.
Others believed the cycle itself was necessary.
No [[Choice Wielder]] ever agreed completely with those who came before.
Developer Note
[[The Black Sword]] should function almost like a library.
Instead of simply speaking,
different previous Wielders should occasionally disagree with one another.
This makes the sword feel alive without actually being sentient.
