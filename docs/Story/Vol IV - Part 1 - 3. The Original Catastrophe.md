# 3. The Original Catastrophe
**Volume**: [[Volume IV – The Core and the Final Choice]] | **Part**: [[Part 1 – The Truth of the Core]]

---

[[The Core]] did not grant wishes.
It simply amplified intent.
When humanity attempted to rewrite reality, [[The Core|the Core]] obeyed.
Reality fractured.
Time splintered.
Space collapsed.
Life and Death lost balance.
Creation became uncontrollable.
Chaos spread across existence.
Entire civilizations disappeared in moments.
The world nearly ended.
This became the First Collapse.
