# 8. One Last Question
**Volume**: [[Volume IV – The Core and the Final Choice]] | **Part**: [[Part 2 – The Burden of the Seven]]

---

The Seven stand.
[[The Core]] falls silent.
Reality itself waits.
[[Chronar]] asks the final question.
Not as a Primordial.
As a man.
"[[Christ]]..."
"If you could free us..."
"...what would you choose?"
The screen fades to white.
No answer is given.
The player has reached the point where the story no longer belongs to the writers.
It belongs to them.
