# 2. Why the Realms Exist
**Volume**: [[Volume II – The Child of Choice]] | **Part**: [[Part 4 – The Seven Realms]]

---

When the Seven became Primordials, they realized something dangerous.
The Fundamental Laws could not simply exist within themselves.
They needed places where those laws could remain stable.
To solve this, each Primordial shaped a Realm around their Law.
These Realms became anchors for reality.
The Primordials do not rule these Realms like kings.
Instead, they maintain them.
They spend almost all of their existence ensuring that their Realm remains balanced.
This is why they rarely interact with humanity.
Leaving a Realm unattended for too long risks destabilizing the Law itself.
