# Part 4 – The Seven Realms
**Parent Volume**: [[Volume II – The Child of Choice]]

## Introduction
Purpose of this Part
This section concludes the prologue of the game. It introduces the concept of the [[Seven Realms]], explains why the
Primordials created them, why [[Christ]] must clear them in a specific order, and establishes the true objective of the
game's progression.
By the end of this part, the player should understand one thing:
The Seven are not trying to stop [[Christ]]. They are trying to determine whether he deserves to learn the truth.

## Key Takeaways
• The [[Seven Realms]] are manifestations of the Fundamental Laws and act as anchors for reality.
• Each Realm contains a corrupted Realm Spirit that must be defeated to restore balance.
• The Primordials are not the game's bosses; they serve as mentors, judges, and witnesses.
• Every Realm is both a gameplay challenge and a philosophical lesson about the Law it represents.
• [[Christ]]'s certainty that the Seven are villains slowly begins to crumble.
• By the end of Volume II, [[Christ]] reaches [[The Core|the Core]]—not as a vengeful son, but as someone searching for the
truth.
