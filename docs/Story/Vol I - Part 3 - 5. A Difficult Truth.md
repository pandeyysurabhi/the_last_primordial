# 5. A Difficult Truth
**Volume**: [[Volume I – The Birth of Reality]] | **Part**: [[Part 3 – The Seven Primordials]]

---

As centuries passed, the Seven noticed something deeply troubling.
Humanity had learned nothing.
[[The Core]] had been sealed away, yet human nature remained unchanged.
Kingdoms still fought wars.
People still sought power.
Civilizations still believed they alone possessed the correct vision for the future.
The Seven realized that if [[The Core|the Core]] were ever discovered again, history would repeat itself.
The problem had never been [[The Core|the Core]].
The problem was that humanity always believed it could perfectly control reality.
