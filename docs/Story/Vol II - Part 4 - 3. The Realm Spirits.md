# 3. The Realm Spirits
**Volume**: [[Volume II – The Child of Choice]] | **Part**: [[Part 4 – The Seven Realms]]

---

Each Realm possesses a guardian.
These are known as Realm Spirits.
Realm Spirits are not the Primordials.
They are manifestations born from the Realm itself.

Originally, they existed to help maintain balance and prevent corruption within their respective Laws.
Over thousands of years, however, they absorbed the instability caused by humanity's repeated interference with
reality.
Instead of protecting balance, they gradually became twisted reflections of their own Laws.
For example:
• The [[Spirit of Time]] no longer values the natural flow of time—it seeks absolute control over every moment.
• The [[Spirit of Life]] refuses to let anything die, creating endless suffering.
• The [[Spirit of Death]] believes all existence should end equally.
• The [[Spirit of Chaos]] rejects all order, believing uncertainty should consume reality.
Each Spirit represents what happens when a Fundamental Law loses its balance.
Developer Note
The Realm Spirits are the game's primary bosses.
Unlike the Primordials, they are meant to be defeated.
Each boss should demonstrate both the beauty and the danger of its respective Law.
