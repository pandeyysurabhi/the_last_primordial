# 6. The Execution
**Volume**: [[Volume II – The Child of Choice]] | **Part**: [[Part 2 – The Judgment of the Seven]]

---

The Seven descended upon the village.
No battle took place.
No innocent people were harmed.
The Primordials did not come as conquerors.
They came as executioners carrying out a sentence they wished had never been necessary.
[[Christ]]'s parents accepted their fate.
Before his final breath...
[[Christ]]'s father entrusted [[The Black Sword|the Black Sword]] to reality itself.
Rather than leaving it behind,
he released it from his own control.
The sword disappeared.
Waiting...
for the day [[Christ]] would be ready to inherit it.
The Seven carried out the sentence.
By sunrise...
both parents were gone.
