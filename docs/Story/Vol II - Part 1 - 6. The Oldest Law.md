# 6. The Oldest Law
**Volume**: [[Volume II – The Child of Choice]] | **Part**: [[Part 1 – The Impossible Child]]

---

Long before history began...
the Seven established one absolute law.
No bearer of a Fundamental Law may pass their burden through blood.
The reason was simple.
The burden of reality must always be accepted willingly.
Never inherited.
If Primordials could have children...
those children would inherit unimaginable responsibility without ever choosing it.
The Seven believed forcing eternity upon someone was the greatest injustice imaginable.
This became the oldest law in existence.
No exceptions.
