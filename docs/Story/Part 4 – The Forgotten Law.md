# Part 4 – The Forgotten Law
**Parent Volume**: [[Volume I – The Birth of Reality]]

## Introduction
Purpose of this Part
This section concludes the history of the ancient world. It explains why the [[Seven Primordials]] eventually discovered
the existence of an Eighth Fundamental Law, why they could never become its guardians, and how their attempt to
preserve reality unintentionally began a cycle that would continue for thousands of years. This volume ends just
before the birth of [[Christ]].

The Seven discover the existence of an Eighth Law called [[Choice]].
[[Choice]] cannot become a permanent Fundamental Law because it rejects ownership.
[[Choice]] repeatedly manifests through different individuals across history.
Every [[Choice Wielder]] inherits the memories of previous Wielders through [[The Black Sword|the Black Sword]].
The Seven do not view [[Choice Wielder|Choice Wielders]] as enemies; they see them as humanity's final hope.
For ten thousand years, every [[Choice Wielder]] fails to end the cycle.
