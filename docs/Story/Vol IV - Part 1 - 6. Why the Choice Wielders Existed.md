# 6. Why the Choice Wielders Existed
**Volume**: [[Volume IV – The Core and the Final Choice]] | **Part**: [[Part 1 – The Truth of the Core]]

---

After restoring reality, the Seven immediately understood something horrifying.

Reality was stable.
But it was not healthy.
Humanity still possessed free will.
One day...
someone would attempt to control [[The Core|the Core]] again.
The Seven could prevent disasters.
But they could not remove humanity's ability to choose.
Doing so would destroy what made humanity human.
They needed another solution.
Not rulers.
Not soldiers.
Observers.
People capable of understanding both humanity and the Primordials.
Thus began the cycle of the [[Choice Wielder|Choice Wielders]].
Each generation would inherit [[The Black Sword|the Black Sword]].
Each would search for a better future.
Not because the Seven expected success...
but because they refused to abandon hope.
