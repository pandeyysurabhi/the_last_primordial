# 2. The Black Sword's Last Purpose
**Volume**: [[Volume IV – The Core and the Final Choice]] | **Part**: [[Part 3 – The Three Choices]]

---

[[The Black Sword]] begins breaking apart.
Its black metal slowly turns into white fragments of light.
Every previous [[Choice Wielder]] appears around [[Christ]].
Not as ghosts.
As memories finally set free.
Some smile proudly.
Some still regret their choices.
Some remain uncertain.
One by one, they thank [[Christ]].
Not because he succeeded.
But because he reached the place none of them truly understood.
Finally...
his father appears.
He embraces [[Christ]].
No words.
No grand speech.
Just a father seeing his son one last time.
He fades peacefully.
[[The Black Sword]] completely disappears.
The burden of every previous [[Choice Wielder]] ends here.
From this point onward...
[[Christ]] stands entirely alone.
