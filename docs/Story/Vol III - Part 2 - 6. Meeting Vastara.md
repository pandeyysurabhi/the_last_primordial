# 6. Meeting Vastara
**Volume**: [[Volume III – The Pilgrimage Through the Seven Realms]] | **Part**: [[Part 2 – The Realms of Space and Life]]

---

After defeating the Spirit, reality begins unfolding naturally again.
Floating islands return to stable positions.
Gravity normalizes.
The Realm breathes once more.
[[Vastara]] appears.
She watches [[Christ]] quietly.
Then asks:
"If you could place every person exactly where they belong...
would anyone still be free?"
[[Christ]] immediately answers:
"No."
[[Vastara]] smiles for the first time.
Without another word...
she disappears.
This is the first Primordial who neither challenges nor criticizes him.
Instead...
she allows him to discover the answer himself.
