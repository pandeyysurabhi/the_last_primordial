# 5. An Impossible Love
**Volume**: [[Volume II – The Child of Choice]] | **Part**: [[Part 1 – The Impossible Child]]

---

Centuries later...
the [[Immortal Woman|immortal woman]] met the [[Last Choice Wielder]].
He already knew her history.
She knew who he really was.
Neither approached the other because of destiny.
They simply found someone capable of understanding a burden that no ordinary person could.
Their relationship grew slowly.
Not through adventure.
Not through prophecy.
Through conversations.
Together...
they discussed every previous [[Choice Wielder]].
Every failed solution.
Every sacrifice.
Every mistake humanity had repeated throughout history.
Eventually...
they arrived at a terrifying realization.
The cycle itself was the problem.
Every [[Choice Wielder]] entered the story too late.

They inherited thousands of years of failure.
By the time they understood reality...
the cycle had already claimed another victim.
