# 3. Humanity's Rise
**Volume**: [[Volume I – The Birth of Reality]] | **Part**: [[Part 1 – The Beginning of Everything]]

---

Thousands of years passed. Life evolved. Eventually humanity became the dominant civilization. Unlike every species
before it, humanity possessed curiosity. Instead of merely surviving, humans questioned everything around them.
They built cities. Created kingdoms. Studied nature. Developed science. Recorded history. For the first time, a species
attempted to understand reality itself. This curiosity became humanity's greatest strength. It would also become its
greatest weakness.
