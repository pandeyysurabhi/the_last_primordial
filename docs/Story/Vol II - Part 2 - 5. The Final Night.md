# 5. The Final Night
**Volume**: [[Volume II – The Child of Choice]] | **Part**: [[Part 2 – The Judgment of the Seven]]

---

[[Christ]]'s parents knew the Seven were coming.
They did not run.
They did not fight.
They had expected this day from the moment [[Christ]] was conceived.
That night...
they placed their sleeping son in his bed.

His mother kissed his forehead.
His father looked at him one final time.
Neither regretted their decision.
They believed they had already succeeded.
Not because they had changed the world...
but because they had given it a possibility that had never existed before.
