# 3. The Verdict
**Volume**: [[Volume II – The Child of Choice]] | **Part**: [[Part 2 – The Judgment of the Seven]]

---

The Seven reached a unanimous decision.
The law itself could not be broken.
[[Christ]]'s parents had knowingly violated the oldest rule of reality.
If they survived...
every future Law Bearer could justify breaking the same law.
The balance they had protected for ten thousand years would begin to collapse.
The sentence was final.
Both parents would die.
The child would live.
