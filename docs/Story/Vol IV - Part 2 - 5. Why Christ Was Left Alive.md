# 5. Why Christ Was Left Alive
**Volume**: [[Volume IV – The Core and the Final Choice]] | **Part**: [[Part 2 – The Burden of the Seven]]

---

[[Aetherion]] finally answers the question [[Christ]] had carried since childhood.
"We never spared you because you were special."
"We spared you...
because you had never chosen anything."
[[Choice]].
That was always the point.
The Seven refused to decide [[Christ]]'s future.
Only [[Christ]] himself could do that.
