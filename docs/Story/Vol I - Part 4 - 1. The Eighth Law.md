# 1. The Eighth Law
**Volume**: [[Volume I – The Birth of Reality]] | **Part**: [[Part 4 – The Forgotten Law]]

---

For centuries, the Seven believed reality was governed by only [[Universal Laws of Reality|seven fundamental laws]].
Creation.
Destruction.
Time.
Space.
Life.
Death.
Chaos.
Everything appeared balanced.
Yet something continued to trouble them.
Humanity always made different decisions.
History never repeated itself exactly.
Even [[Chronar]], who experienced every [[Timeline|timeline]] simultaneously, occasionally encountered possibilities that could not
be predicted.
Something existed beyond Time itself.
