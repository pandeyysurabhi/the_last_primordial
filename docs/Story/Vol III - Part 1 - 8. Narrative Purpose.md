# 8. Narrative Purpose
**Volume**: [[Volume III – The Pilgrimage Through the Seven Realms]] | **Part**: [[Part 1 – The Realm of Time]]

---

The [[Realm of Time]] establishes the structure of the entire game.
Each Realm follows the same progression:
• Enter the Realm.
• Learn its gameplay mechanics.
• Discover fragments of lore.
• Defeat the corrupted Realm Spirit.
• Speak with the Primordial.
• Leave with more questions than answers.
The player's understanding of the world grows alongside [[Christ]]'s.
