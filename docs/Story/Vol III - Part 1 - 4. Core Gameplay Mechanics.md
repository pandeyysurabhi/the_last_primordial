# 4. Core Gameplay Mechanics
**Volume**: [[Volume III – The Pilgrimage Through the Seven Realms]] | **Part**: [[Part 1 – The Realm of Time]]

---

Throughout the Realm, time continuously shifts.
Mechanics include:
Accelerated Time
Plants instantly grow into climbable vines.
Bridges decay and collapse.
Enemies rapidly age into weaker forms.
Reversed Time
Destroyed mechanisms repair themselves.
Broken doors become intact.
Certain enemies return after defeat.
Frozen Time
Projectiles stop midair.
Moving platforms become stationary.
Water becomes solid.
The player can use frozen moments to solve puzzles.
Fractured Time
Different areas operate at different speeds simultaneously.
One room may be completely frozen while another moves hundreds of times faster.
Players must understand the flow of each area rather than relying on instinct.
