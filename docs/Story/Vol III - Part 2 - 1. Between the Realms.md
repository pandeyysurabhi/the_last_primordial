# 1. Between the Realms
**Volume**: [[Volume III – The Pilgrimage Through the Seven Realms]] | **Part**: [[Part 2 – The Realms of Space and Life]]

---

After restoring the [[Realm of Time]], [[Christ]] notices a change within [[The Black Sword|the Black Sword]].
The voices become calmer.
The previous [[Choice Wielder|Choice Wielders]] no longer constantly argue.
Instead, they begin discussing the choices [[Christ]] makes.
Some praise his compassion.
Others criticize his hesitation.
For the first time, [[Christ]] realizes the Sword is not judging him.
It is observing him.
Every decision he makes becomes part of the legacy that future [[Choice Wielder|Choice Wielders]] may one day inherit.
Developer Note
From this point onward, player choices should subtly influence dialogue from [[The Black Sword|the Black Sword]].
Even though the main story remains the same, the Sword should remember whether the player solved situations
through mercy, aggression, sacrifice, or pragmatism.
This makes the player feel that their philosophy is becoming part of the Sword's history.
