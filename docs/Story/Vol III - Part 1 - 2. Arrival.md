# 2. Arrival
**Volume**: [[Volume III – The Pilgrimage Through the Seven Realms]] | **Part**: [[Part 1 – The Realm of Time]]

---

The entrance to the [[Realm of Time]] appears beneath the ruins of an ancient observatory.
As [[Christ]] approaches, [[The Black Sword|the Black Sword]] begins vibrating.
The previous [[Choice Wielder|Choice Wielders]] become unusually quiet.
One finally speaks.

"Every one of us began here."
The gateway opens.
[[Christ]] steps through.
Immediately...
the sky changes.
The sun rises.
Sets.
Rises again.
Entire days pass within seconds.
Then everything freezes.
Time no longer behaves consistently.
