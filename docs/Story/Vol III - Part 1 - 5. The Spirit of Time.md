# 5. The Spirit of Time
**Volume**: [[Volume III – The Pilgrimage Through the Seven Realms]] | **Part**: [[Part 1 – The Realm of Time]]

---

At the center of the Realm waits the [[Spirit of Time]].

Unlike [[Chronar]], the Spirit believes time should never change.
It seeks perfect order.
Perfect predictability.
To it...
every unexpected event is a flaw.
It attempts to trap [[Christ]] inside repeating timelines where every decision leads to the same outcome.
Its philosophy is simple:
"If nothing changes...
nothing can go wrong."
This represents Time taken to its absolute extreme.
Boss Mechanics
The battle itself constantly changes.
• The arena rewinds after certain attacks.
• Defeated enemies return.
• [[Christ]]'s previous actions are replayed as ghostly echoes that can either help or hinder him.
• Health lost during one phase may return in another as time reverses.
Victory requires adapting rather than memorizing attack patterns.
