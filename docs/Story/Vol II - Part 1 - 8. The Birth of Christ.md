# 8. The Birth of Christ
**Volume**: [[Volume II – The Child of Choice]] | **Part**: [[Part 1 – The Impossible Child]]

---

Despite knowing the consequences...
they made their decision.
Not out of selfishness.
Not out of rebellion.
But because they believed the future deserved one final chance.
Months later...
a child was born.
They named him...
[[Christ]].
Nothing extraordinary happened that day.
The sky remained clear.
The earth did not tremble.
No prophecy announced his arrival.
To the world...
he was simply another child.
Only one thing was different.
For the first time in ten thousand years...
[[Choice]] had not selected its next Wielder.
[[Choice]] had been born.
