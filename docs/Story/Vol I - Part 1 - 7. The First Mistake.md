# 7. The First Mistake
**Volume**: [[Volume I – The Birth of Reality]] | **Part**: [[Part 1 – The Beginning of Everything]]

---

History often describes humanity's downfall as greed. That is incorrect. Humanity did not begin by seeking power. It
began by trying to eliminate suffering. One kingdom asked: "Can we cure every disease?" Another asked: "Can we
prevent every natural disaster?" Another asked: "Can death itself be removed?" None of these questions were evil.
Each was born from compassion. The problem was something humanity had not yet understood. Reality is not built
from independent rules. Every law depends on every other. Removing one form of suffering often creates another.
Changing one law changes countless others. [[The Core]] never refused these requests. It simply responded to
humanity's intentions. And that was enough to begin breaking reality.
