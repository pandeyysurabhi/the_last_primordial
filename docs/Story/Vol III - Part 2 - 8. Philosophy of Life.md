# 8. Philosophy of Life
**Volume**: [[Volume III – The Pilgrimage Through the Seven Realms]] | **Part**: [[Part 2 – The Realms of Space and Life]]

---

Life exists because it changes.
Birth.
Growth.
Decay.
Death.
Renewal.
These are not separate events.
They are one continuous cycle.
The [[Spirit of Life]] forgot this truth.

It came to believe that preserving life at any cost was true kindness.
That belief slowly corrupted the Realm.
