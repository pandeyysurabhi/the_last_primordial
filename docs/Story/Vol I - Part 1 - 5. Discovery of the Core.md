# 5. Discovery of the Core
**Volume**: [[Volume I – The Birth of Reality]] | **Part**: [[Part 1 – The Beginning of Everything]]

---

Everything changed during an archaeological expedition beneath the oldest mountain range in the world. Deep
underground, explorers discovered ruins far older than humanity itself. At the center of those ruins rested [[The Core|the Core]].
No one understood what it was. No language could describe it.It emitted no light.No heat. No sound. Yet everyone
who stood near it instinctively felt that it was older than existence itself. Years of research revealed something
extraordinary. [[The Core]] responded to human interaction. Not through words. Not through commands. But through
intent. Humanity had unknowingly discovered the foundation of reality.
