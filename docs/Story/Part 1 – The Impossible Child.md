# Part 1 – The Impossible Child
**Parent Volume**: [[Volume II – The Child of Choice]]

## Introduction
Purpose of this Part
This volume shifts the focus from the history of the world to the beginning of the protagonist's story.
It explains who [[Christ]]'s parents were, why they broke the oldest law of reality, why the [[Seven Primordials]] were
forced to execute them, and why [[Christ]] became the most important person in history before he was even old enough
to understand the world.
This section should make one thing clear:
[[Christ]] was never destined to save the world. He was born because two people believed the world deserved
another chance.

## Key Takeaways
• The [[Last Choice Wielder]] understood that the Primordials were victims, not villains.
• [[Morvex]]'s love for a mortal woman created the first crack in the ancient laws.
• The Seven chose mercy instead of punishment, showing that they remained compassionate despite their
burdens.
• The [[Last Choice Wielder]] and the [[Immortal Woman|immortal woman]] concluded that the endless cycle itself was the true
enemy.
• They deliberately broke the oldest law by having a child.
• [[Christ]] became the first person in history to be born with the essence of [[Choice]], making his existence
unprecedented and unknowingly setting the stage for the greatest crisis the Seven would ever face.
