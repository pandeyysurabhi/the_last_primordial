# 6. The Final Council
**Volume**: [[Volume I – The Birth of Reality]] | **Part**: [[Part 2 – The Collapse of Reality]]

---

As reality deteriorated, the [[Seven Kingdoms]] abandoned their rivalries.
Representatives from every nation gathered near [[The Core|the Core]].

This meeting later became known as The Final Council.
Hundreds of proposals were discussed.
Destroy [[The Core|the Core]].
Seal [[The Core|the Core]].
Reverse every experiment.
Abandon civilization entirely.
None worked.
The damage had progressed beyond repair.
Reality no longer possessed enough stability to heal itself.
For the first time in history, humanity faced extinction.
