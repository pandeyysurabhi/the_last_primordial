# 2. The Core
**Volume**: [[Volume I – The Birth of Reality]] | **Part**: [[Part 1 – The Beginning of Everything]]

---

[[The Core]] is the source from which every fundamental law of existence emerged. It never created these laws
intentionally. They formed naturally as reality stabilized. These laws became:
•
•
•
•

Creation
Destruction
Time
Space
Life
Death
Chaos
These are not magical powers. They are the principles that allow reality to exist. Every moment, these laws work
together. Life cannot exist without Death. Creation has no meaning without Destruction. Time cannot move without
Space. Chaos allows change, preventing existence from becoming completely stagnant. If even one law disappears,
reality begins to collapse. This balance is the foundation of the universe.
Developer Note
The [[Seven Primordials]] did not create these laws. They only became their guardians much later. This distinction is
important because the Primordials are caretakers, not gods.
