# 5. The Price of Becoming a Primordial
**Volume**: [[Volume IV – The Core and the Final Choice]] | **Part**: [[Part 1 – The Truth of the Core]]

---

[[The Core]] demanded one condition.
A Fundamental Law could never belong to someone seeking personal happiness.
The Seven surrendered everything.
They could never grow old.
They could never abandon their Law.
They could never truly live as humans again.
Their names became part of reality itself.
Their emotions slowly faded beneath endless responsibility.
The burden they accepted was never meant to be temporary.
It was eternal.
