# 4. The Volunteers
**Volume**: [[Volume IV – The Core and the Final Choice]] | **Part**: [[Part 1 – The Truth of the Core]]

---

As the First Collapse consumed reality, seven ordinary humans stepped forward.
Not kings.
Not gods.
Not chosen heroes.
Simply people willing to sacrifice everything.
Each accepted responsibility for stabilizing one Fundamental Law.
Not because they desired power...
but because someone had to.
[[The Core]] accepted their decision.
From that moment onward...
they ceased to be ordinary humans.
They became the [[Seven Primordials]].
