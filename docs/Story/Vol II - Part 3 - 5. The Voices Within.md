# 5. The Voices Within
**Volume**: [[Volume II – The Child of Choice]] | **Part**: [[Part 3 – The Black Sword and the Journey Begins]]

---

[[Christ]] did not gain power immediately.
Instead...
he heard voices.
Dozens.
Hundreds.
Then thousands.
Each belonged to a previous [[Choice Wielder]].
None spoke at once.
Instead, they argued.
One believed humanity should never have touched [[The Core|the Core]].
Another insisted the Primordials had ruled for too long.
Some regretted their choices.
Others stood by them.
One voice laughed.
Another cried.
Some begged [[Christ]] to abandon the sword.
Others urged him to continue the fight they could not finish.
The sword did not command him.
It offered perspectives.
Every [[Choice Wielder]] had left behind their memories, doubts, and beliefs.
[[Christ]] realized that he was no longer alone.
Developer Note
The previous Wielders should never function as quest markers or tutorial characters.

Think of them as philosophers.
Sometimes they help.
Sometimes they contradict one another.
Occasionally, they are completely wrong.
The player should decide whose ideas to trust.
