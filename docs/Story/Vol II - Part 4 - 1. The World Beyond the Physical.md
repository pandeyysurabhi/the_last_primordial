# 1. The World Beyond the Physical
**Volume**: [[Volume II – The Child of Choice]] | **Part**: [[Part 4 – The Seven Realms]]

---

Reality exists on two different levels.
The first is the Mortal Realm, where humanity lives.
The second consists of the [[Seven Realms]], each representing one Fundamental Law.
These Realms are not planets or dimensions created by magic.
They are manifestations of reality itself.
Every Primordial is connected to one Realm.
If a Realm collapses, the corresponding Law weakens.
If a Law weakens, reality itself begins to fracture.
Developer Note
The [[Seven Realms]] should each have a completely different visual identity. Players should instantly recognize which
Law they are inside without needing dialogue.
