# 2. The Realm of Space
**Volume**: [[Volume III – The Pilgrimage Through the Seven Realms]] | **Part**: [[Part 2 – The Realms of Space and Life]]

---

The entrance to the second Realm lies inside an abandoned temple that appears much larger on the inside than the
outside.
As [[Christ]] steps through the gateway, distance loses meaning.
There is no horizon.
No fixed direction.
The sky folds into itself.
Mountains float upside down.
Entire islands drift through empty space.
Roads twist into impossible shapes.
Every location exists...
yet none remain where they should.
