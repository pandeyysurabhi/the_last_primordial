# 1. The Seven Enter the Core
**Volume**: [[Volume IV – The Core and the Final Choice]] | **Part**: [[Part 2 – The Burden of the Seven]]

---

One by one...
the Seven walk into [[The Core|the Core]].
Not as rulers.
Not as judges.
Simply as seven ordinary people.
For the first time...
their divine appearances disappear.
Their crowns fade.
Their robes vanish.
Their immense power dissolves into white light.
Standing before [[Christ]] are the same seven humans who volunteered during the First Collapse.
Older.
Tired.
Broken.
Yet still standing.
