# 7. The Decision
**Volume**: [[Volume II – The Child of Choice]] | **Part**: [[Part 3 – The Black Sword and the Journey Begins]]

---

[[The Black Sword]] did not force [[Christ]] to leave.
It simply revealed fragments of the truth.
Someone had murdered his parents.
Someone powerful enough to erase the truth from history.
The previous Wielders knew the answer.
One name appeared repeatedly within their memories.
The [[Seven Primordials]].
[[Christ]] reached the only conclusion that made sense to him.
The Seven had destroyed his family.
He would destroy them in return.
He packed only the essentials.
[[The Black Sword]].
A small bag of supplies.
A pendant that once belonged to his mother.
Without telling anyone...
he walked out of the village.
He believed he was beginning a journey of revenge.
In reality...
he had just stepped into a journey designed to challenge everything he believed.
