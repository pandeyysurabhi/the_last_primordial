# 6. The Burden Ends
**Volume**: [[Volume IV – The Core and the Final Choice]] | **Part**: [[Part 2 – The Burden of the Seven]]

---

For the first time in ten thousand years...
the Seven kneel.
Not in worship.
Not in surrender.
In relief.
[[Chronar]] speaks.
"We have reached the end of our responsibility."
[[Morvex]] quietly closes his eyes.
"We protected reality until someone capable of making a new [[Choice|choice]] arrived."
[[Elaria]] smiles through tears.

"You."
None of them ask [[Christ]] to replace them.
None ask him to save them.
For the first time...
they ask nothing.
