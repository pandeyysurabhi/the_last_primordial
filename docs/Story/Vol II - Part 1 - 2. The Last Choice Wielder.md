# 2. The Last Choice Wielder
**Volume**: [[Volume II – The Child of Choice]] | **Part**: [[Part 1 – The Impossible Child]]

---

The final [[Choice Wielder]] before [[Christ]] was different.
Unlike his predecessors, he never saw the Primordials as enemies.
Instead of searching for a way to defeat them, he spent years understanding them.
He observed [[Chronar]]'s endless isolation.
He witnessed [[Morvex]] experience every death across existence.
He watched [[Elaria]] mourn countless lives she could never save.
He realized something no previous Wielder had fully accepted.
The Seven were prisoners.
They had sacrificed everything to save humanity...
and humanity remembered them only as gods or monsters.
For the first time in history, a [[Choice Wielder]] questioned whether the Seven needed saving just as much as humanity
did.
Developer Note
The [[Last Choice Wielder]] should never be portrayed as a failed hero.
Instead, he is a philosopher.
He asks questions no one else has considered.
His greatest strength is not his combat ability.
It is his understanding of people.
