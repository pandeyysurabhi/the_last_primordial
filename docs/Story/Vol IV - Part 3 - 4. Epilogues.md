# 4. Epilogues
**Volume**: [[Volume IV – The Core and the Final Choice]] | **Part**: [[Part 3 – The Three Choices]]

---

Each ending includes a short epilogue showing the world's future.
Eternal Guardian
Generations pass.
Legends speak of a silent figure watching over reality.
No one remembers his name.
But every sunrise exists because of his [[Choice|choice]].
Age of Humanity
The world becomes unpredictable.
Some centuries are peaceful.
Others are filled with conflict.
Yet every achievement...
and every mistake...
belongs entirely to humanity.
The stars shine without anyone guiding them.
Broken Cycle
Years later...
children study the story of the Seven not as gods...
but as the first people who taught humanity that responsibility should be shared.
The Primordials live among ordinary people as mentors.
[[Christ]] finally visits his parents' grave.
Not as a warrior.
Not as a savior.
Simply as their son.

He smiles.
For the first time since childhood.
