# 12. Christ Begins to Doubt
**Volume**: [[Volume III – The Pilgrimage Through the Seven Realms]] | **Part**: [[Part 2 – The Realms of Space and Life]]

---

Three Realms.
Three Primordials.
None have attacked him.
None have defended themselves.
None have justified killing his parents.
Instead...
they continue asking questions.
Questions that force [[Christ]] to examine his own beliefs.
For the first time...
he wonders whether revenge is enough.
The hatred remains.
But certainty begins to fade.
