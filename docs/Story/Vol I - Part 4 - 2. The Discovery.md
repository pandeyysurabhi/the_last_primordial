# 2. The Discovery
**Volume**: [[Volume I – The Birth of Reality]] | **Part**: [[Part 4 – The Forgotten Law]]

---

After centuries of studying [[The Core|the Core]], the Seven reached an impossible conclusion.
Reality contained an eighth law.
Unlike every other law, it possessed no constant form.
It could not be measured.
It could not be stabilized.
It could not be inherited.
It existed wherever free will existed.
The Seven named it...
[[Choice]].
Unlike the other laws, [[Choice]] refused to belong to anyone.
It constantly changed with every decision made by every living being.
Developer Note
[[Choice]] is not "good."
It is also not "evil."
[[Choice]] is simply the possibility that reality may take a different path.
Without [[Choice]], progress cannot exist.
Without [[Choice]], mistakes also cannot exist.
