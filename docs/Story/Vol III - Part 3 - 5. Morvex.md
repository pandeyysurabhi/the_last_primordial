# 5. Morvex
**Volume**: [[Volume III – The Pilgrimage Through the Seven Realms]] | **Part**: [[Part 3 – Death, Chaos, and the Cracks in Memory]]

---

After the Spirit is freed, the Realm becomes brighter.
The wandering souls begin disappearing peacefully.
[[Morvex]] appears.
For the first time...
he looks directly at [[Christ]].
His expression is filled with guilt.
He asks only one question.

"Do you hate me...
because I brought death to your family...
or because death reached them at all?"
[[Christ]] cannot answer.
Before leaving, [[Morvex]] quietly says:
"One day...
you'll know why I could never save them."
Those words haunt [[Christ]] long after the Realm fades.
