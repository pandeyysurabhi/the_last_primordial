# Part 1 – The Beginning of Everything
**Parent Volume**: [[Volume I – The Birth of Reality]]

## Introduction
Purpose of this Volume
This volume establishes the foundation of the game's universe. It explains how reality works, why the Seven
Primordials exist, what [[The Core|the Core]] is, and how humanity unknowingly became responsible for the world's downfall.
Understanding this history is essential before introducing [[Christ]] or the events of the game.

## Key Takeaways

[[The Core]] predates the universe and is the foundation of reality.
The [[Universal Laws of Reality|Seven Fundamental Laws]] existed before the Primordials.
The Primordials are guardians, not creators.
Humanity entered a Golden Age through knowledge and cooperation.
The discovery of [[The Core|the Core]] accelerated civilization beyond imagination.
Humanity's downfall began not from evil, but from the sincere desire to create a perfect world.
Every attempt to remove suffering unintentionally weakened the balance of reality, setting the stage for the
collapse explored in Part 2.
