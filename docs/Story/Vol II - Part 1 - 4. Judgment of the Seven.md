# 4. Judgment of the Seven
**Volume**: [[Volume II – The Child of Choice]] | **Part**: [[Part 1 – The Impossible Child]]

---

The other Primordials immediately discovered what [[Morvex]] had done.
This was the first time one of them had used their authority for personal reasons.
The consequences could have been catastrophic.
If Primordials began changing reality according to emotion...
they would become exactly what humanity once tried to become.
Rulers of existence.
The Seven gathered.
For the first time in thousands of years...
they argued with one another.
Some demanded punishment.
Others understood [[Morvex]]'s loneliness.
In the end...
they reached a unanimous decision.
The woman would be allowed to live.
But [[Morvex]] would never again interfere with the natural cycle for personal reasons.
The incident was sealed from history.
Only the Seven remembered it.
Developer Note
This decision is important.
The Seven are not emotionless.
They are capable of compassion.
Their greatest strength is also their greatest weakness.
They understand suffering because they constantly experience it themselves.
