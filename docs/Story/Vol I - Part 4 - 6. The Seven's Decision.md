# 6. The Seven's Decision
**Volume**: [[Volume I – The Birth of Reality]] | **Part**: [[Part 4 – The Forgotten Law]]

---

The Seven never hunted the [[Choice Wielder|Choice Wielders]].
They observed them.
Each time,
they hoped someone would discover a solution they themselves had overlooked.
Every [[Choice Wielder]] eventually reached the same conclusion.

Reality could not continue like this.
But none could agree on how to save it.
Some died protecting humanity.
Some died opposing the Seven.
Some disappeared completely.
History remembered none of them.
The cycle continued.
Again.
Again.
Again.
For nearly ten thousand years.
