# 1. The Heart of Reality
**Volume**: [[Volume IV – The Core and the Final Choice]] | **Part**: [[Part 1 – The Truth of the Core]]

---

[[The Core]] is unlike anything [[Christ]] imagined.
There are no walls.
No sky.
No floor.
It exists outside Time, Space, Life, and every other Fundamental Law.
It appears as an endless sea of white light, with fragments of memories floating like stars.
Every memory belongs to someone.
Some belong to ordinary humans.
Others belong to the Primordials.
Others belong to [[Choice Wielder|Choice Wielders]] who lived thousands of years ago.
[[The Core]] does not speak.
It remembers.
Developer Note
[[The Core]] should feel sacred, not threatening.
The player should feel as though they have entered a place no mortal was ever meant to see.
