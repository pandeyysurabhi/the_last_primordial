# Part 1 – The Truth of the Core
**Parent Volume**: [[Volume IV – The Core and the Final Choice]]

## Introduction
Purpose of this Part
This section reveals the complete truth behind reality.
[[Christ]] finally learns why the Seven became Primordials, why the [[Choice Wielder|Choice Wielders]] always failed, why his parents
sacrificed themselves, and what [[The Core|the Core]] actually is.
Most importantly, it reveals that [[The Core|the Core]] is not the villain.
It is merely doing exactly what it was created to do.

## Key Takeaways
• [[The Core]] is revealed as the memory and mirror of reality, not its ruler.
• The Seven were ordinary humans who willingly accepted eternal burdens to save existence during the First
Collapse.
• [[The Core]] never grants power based on worthiness; it simply responds to genuine choices.
• The [[Choice Wielder|Choice Wielders]] were created to seek a solution beyond the Seven's perspective, but every one of them
ultimately failed.
• [[Christ]]'s parents knowingly sacrificed themselves so that someone could be born carrying [[Choice]] instead of
inheriting it later in life.
• [[Christ]] discovers that his purpose is not revenge, but to attempt the one thing no one before him could: break
the cycle itself.
