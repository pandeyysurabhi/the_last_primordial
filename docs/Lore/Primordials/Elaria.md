## The Price of Becoming a Primordial (Narrative Context)
> [!WARNING]
> The Primordials are caretakers of reality, not gods. They carry a heavy personal burden.

Elaria feels every living being.
Every birth.
Every illness.

Every heartbeat.
Every act of love.
Every moment of suffering.
She experiences all of it simultaneously.
Saving one life means witnessing thousands of others die elsewhere.
