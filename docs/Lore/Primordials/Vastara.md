## The Price of Becoming a Primordial (Narrative Context)
> [!WARNING]
> The Primordials are caretakers of reality, not gods. They carry a heavy personal burden.

Vastara constantly maintains the structure of reality.
Every tear in space causes immense pain.
She cannot rest.
Even while speaking to others, part of her consciousness is repairing reality across the universe.
