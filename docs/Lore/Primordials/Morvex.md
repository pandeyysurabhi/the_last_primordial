## The Price of Becoming a Primordial (Narrative Context)
> [!WARNING]
> The Primordials are caretakers of reality, not gods. They carry a heavy personal burden.

Morvex experiences every death.
Every final breath.
Every goodbye.
Every parent losing a child.
Every child losing a parent.
He is not responsible for causing death.
He simply bears witness to every single one.
Over thousands of years, grief becomes his constant companion.
etherion – Creation
Creation never ends.
