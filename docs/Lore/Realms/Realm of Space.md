# Realm of Space
**Category**: [[Seven Realms]] | **Guardian**: [[Vastara]] | **Associated Spirit**: [[Spirit of Space]] | **Associated Kingdom**: [[Kingdom of Space]]

---

The **Realm of Space** is one of the Seven Realms established by the [[Seven Primordials]] as anchors for the Fundamental Laws of reality. During his pilgrimage, [[Christ]] must travel through this realm and defeat its corrupted Spirit.
