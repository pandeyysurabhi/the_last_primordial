## Narrative Backstory
Christ was born as a violation of the oldest law of reality: *no bearer of a Fundamental Law may pass their legacy through blood*. He is the child of the [[Last Choice Wielder]] and the [[Immortal Woman]] (loved and saved by [[Morvex]]). 

Unlike any previous [[Choice Wielder]] who inherited the role, Christ was born carrying the very essence of [[Choice]], making him the ultimate hope to break the ten-thousand-year endless cycle.
