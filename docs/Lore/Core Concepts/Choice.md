# Choice
**Category**: [[Lore Index|Core Concepts]] | **The Forgotten Eighth Law**

---

## Lore
**Choice** is the Eighth Law of reality, discovered by the [[Seven Primordials]] centuries after their ascension. Unlike the other laws, it cannot be owned or stabilized. It has no constant form, rejects permanence, and exists wherever free will exists.

## The Cycle
Because Choice could not be bound to a guardian, it repeatedly manifested through individuals known as [[Choice Wielder|Choice Wielders]] throughout history, leading to a ten-thousand-year cycle of failed attempts to save reality. 

[[Christ]] is the first person in history born with the essence of Choice, allowing him to potentially break the cycle.
