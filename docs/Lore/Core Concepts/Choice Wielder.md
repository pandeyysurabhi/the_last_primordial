# Choice Wielder
**Category**: [[Lore Index|Core Concepts]]

---

## Lore
A **Choice Wielder** is an individual connected to the forgotten law of [[Choice]] who stands at a crucial turning point in history. Throughout ten thousand years, the [[The Black Sword|Black Sword]] would find the next Choice Wielder, letting them inherit the memories and failed philosophies of all who came before.

Each Choice Wielder eventually reached the Core, confronted the [[Seven Primordials]], and failed to end the cycle because they could not find a perfect solution that balanced the laws of reality.
