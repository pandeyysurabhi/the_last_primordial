# The Core
**Category**: [[Lore Index|Core Concepts]]

---

## Lore
The **Core** is the oldest thing in the universe, acting as the engine of reality. It has no emotions, consciousness, or desires, and does not judge good or evil. It simply exists. Destroying the Core would erase the rules of reality itself.

It is the source from which the [[Universal Laws of Reality|Seven Fundamental Laws]] emerged. It responds directly to the intent and choices of sentient beings, which led to the collapse of reality during humanity's Golden Age when they attempted to eliminate all suffering.
